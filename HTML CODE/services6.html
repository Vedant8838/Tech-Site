      • Mission and Vision

    • The company's mission is to promote global well-being through a spirit of exploration and diverse technologies. By leveraging its material intelligence, Mitsui Kinzoku aims to address global challenges and contribute positively to society. Its vision for 2030 is to build new businesses and shape the future with its material expertise.

    • Material Character: Developing new alloys, transforming metals into non-metal materials, and designing metal-resin composites.
    • Material Surface: Specializing in surface treatments, including the development of catalysts with nano-sized active components and coatings that impart specific properties.
    • Material Shape and Size: Excelling in material processing technologies, such as producing ultra-thin electrodeposited copper foil and altering particle shapes and sizes.

    • Abarish Healthcare Pvt. Ltd.

  • Abaris Healthcare Pvt. Ltd., established in July 2005, is a WHO-GMP certified pharmaceutical company headquartered in Delhi, India. The company specializes in manufacturing a wide range of pharmaceutical products, including intravenous (IV) fluids, liquid injections, oral liquids, tablets, and capsules. Their state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities are located in Rajpur, Gujarat, and Kangra, Himachal Pradesh, both adhering to stringent quality standards.

      • Manufacturing Capabilities

    • Advanced Technology: Utilizing aseptic Form-Fill-Seal (FFS) technology from Rommelag, Abaris ensures a manufacturing process with no human touch, maintaining high sterility and quality.
    • Quality Assurance: Equipped with in-house quality control labs featuring top-of-the-line equipment, the company maintains strict adherence to WHO-GMP compliance, ensuring product safety and efficacy.

      • Business Operations

    • Contract Manufacturing: Abaris collaborates with renowned pharmaceutical companies such as Abbott, Alkem, Wockhardt, Intas, Lupin, Biocon, Baxter, Mankind, Hetero, Zydus Cadila, Cadila Pharmaceuticals, Troikaa, and Macleods, offering contract manufacturing services from their WHO-GMP certified facilities.
    • Global Presence: With a significant footprint in the African continent, Abaris has expanded its reach to Europe, Russia, and South America. Their manufacturing facilities have received approvals from regulatory authorities in countries like Uganda, Kenya, Congo, Nepal, Yemen, Nigeria, Ethiopia, Sierra Leone, and Cameroon.
